Intranet Strategy

Nationwide Insurance Intranet Redesign

Problem: An org-chart-centric intranet buried resources across departmental silos, generating repeat support tickets and frustrating employees whose daily tasks crossed team boundaries.

Action: Led user research (12 interviews, 84-person survey) to map navigation failures, proposed a task-based IA, and designed a cross-channel editorial calendar in Miro.

Result: Task-based IA adopted as the new standard, reducing depth-to-resource from 4 clicks to 2. Editorial calendar replaced ad hoc planning process.
